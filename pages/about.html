ABOUT ME

Anduru is a passionate storyteller who pours her heart into every word. Drawing from personal growth and life experiences, she creates authentic connections with readers through raw, reflective writing. Her blog, Heart on a Sleeve, is a space where vulnerability and imperfection are embraced, with the goal of inspiring and building a community where everyone can see a bit of themselves in her stories.
